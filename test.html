(as rerun this year)

Book 1.


So, in book one, the main cast is getting ready for bed after the halloween party. A few of them (Ace and Idia are the ones I can remember) lament the idea of getting up at 6 in the morning to clean the school from top to bottom. Riddle expresses a sense of duty and they all go off to bed eventually. Later, Ace wakes up and tells Duece to stop snoring so loud (spoiler alert he's not-'cause he's not even there!!!) and then Trey comes into the room, freaked out. Ace almost gets possesed by a ghost, but he doesn't know that, and he follows Trey to do a headcount of the people in Heartslaybul at the moment, where they make the horrifying discovery that half of the student body has simply dissapeared. Riddle laments at Cater not being there to lighten the mood and they go off to find the Headmage. Crowley calls a housewarden meeting(though it can hardly be called that with the coolest housewardens missing) and discovers that half of the students are gone. Some students are obviously a bit panicked and Vargas has some common sense for once and says that obviously they are terrified, this is scary. They plan to leave the school using the Dark Mirror, but the mirror starts glowing and sucks all the remaining students into it except for the ones in the main cast (Riddle, Ace, Trey, Leona, Ruggie, Floyd, Jamil, Rook, Ortho, Sebek, and Silver.) They freak out about it for a hot second and the teachers are worried and it's not good. At some point at the end of book one or the beginning of book two they make the ultimate decision that they have to go into the busted mirror.
